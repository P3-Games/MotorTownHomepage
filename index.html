Meet the Vulcan

Meet the Vulcan, our First Forklift! An extra large 25 TON Forklift, designed for the new Ultra Heavy Cargo, Steel Coil! Currently, the Vulcan isn't ownable, but we are working on it! The Vulcan can be found at the Steel mill Coil Factory

Oji Steel Mill

Meet the newest large industry to hit Oji Island, The OJi Steel Mill! Found in the South-East portions of Oji. H-Beam and Coil Production H-Beam and Steel Coil is now produced at the Steel mill. Coal, Iron Ore and Limestone is required for Steel production. Can be delivered to harbor and Formula SCM Factory. (More Factories will be added later)

Gwangjin Iron Mine

The New Gwangjin Iron Ore Mine can be found near the Gwangjin Coal Mine, located on Oji Island. Iron Ore can be quite tricky, as it possesses a randomized weight! Migeum Coal and Iron Ore warehouse is also added. You can stock up coal and iron ore from nearby mines. Dasa harbor now imports Coal and Iron ore

Atlas 8x4 Dump Truck

Enjoy the new Atlas 8x4 Dump Truck, the current largest Dump Truck in the game. It comes weighing in at a whopping 25 Tons, with high level requirements. Can be found at Gosan Truck, in Jeju Island.

The Atlas Family Grows!

The Atlas Family Grows! 3 new semi trucks were added to the Atlas Class. An Atlas 4x2 semi, 6x2 semi and 6x4 semi. Pay attension to which semi truck you choose, as they are different! Can be found at the Gosan Truck, in Jeju Island.

Improved Attachment System

The attachment system has been reworked thanks to suggestions by MrMagic87 and Bob the MadMan. Placing attachment on vehicle now has 1cm/1degree snap. You can tweak location/rotation/light mode in UI after placing it.You can browse all attachment by interacting any attachment
